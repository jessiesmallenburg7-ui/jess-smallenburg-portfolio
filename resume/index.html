Professional Summary

I am a Healthcare UX Researcher and Designer with over twelve years of experience improving complex systems through rigorous user research, usability evaluation, and human-centered design. I recently completed an M.S. in Community Health Education at Wayne State University (Summa Cum Laude), building on my earlier M.S. in Information with a specialization in Human-Computer Interaction from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor.

After working across multiple industries, I found renewed purpose contributing to healthcare and medical projects. I gained direct clinical workflow experience as a volunteer at Michigan Medicine, which deepened my understanding of real-world healthcare environments. I now combine my expertise in usability, human factors, and interaction design with public health knowledge to create better digital experiences for patients, clinicians, and communities.
